Livonian is a Finnic language which means that it descended from the Proto-Finnic language, the same ancestor of Finnish and Estonian.

Livonian is notable for having some rather unusual sound changes compared to other Finnic languages giving it a distinct look and sound. A fairly complete list of sound changes from Proto-Finnic to Proto-Livonian can be found in the paper "Historical phonology From Proto-Finnic to Proto-Livonian".
